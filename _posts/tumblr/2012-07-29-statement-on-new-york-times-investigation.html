Pretty wild double-speak used by PayPal’s Michael Barrett in this post. I am having a hard time reconciling this:

Following the WikiLeaks account restriction in December 2010, we were approached by the U.S. State Department to join a task force to conduct a long term investigation of the New York Times. The task force was created to specifically investigate articles the New York Times published in conjunction with WikiLeaks.
with this:
we at PayPal believe it is right to stand up for journalism, no matter the cost.
Wouldn’t standing up for journalism logically entail resisting a government’s effort to investigate and censor a newspaper for, uh, journalism? I think so.
