There really is no good reason. If people are concerned about kids’ competence as voters, then they ought to push for competency tests for voting. If they worry about parents’ having a disproportionate effect on outcomes, then they ought to consider imposing an age maximum as well to protect against elderly voters’ ability to protect their own interests no matter the cost.
