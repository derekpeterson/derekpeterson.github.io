But another finding of the so-called “soul poll” that researchers said was special for Detroit is the level of attachment that young residents feel for this place.

“Your 18-24-year-olds are your most attached group,” said Gallup researcher Katherine Laughlin. “I can’t tell you what other communities would pay for that finding.”
