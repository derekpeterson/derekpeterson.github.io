Singledom, long on the rise in the developed world, is now starting to catch on in the developing world and in the Middle East. There is not a clear answer whether this is a good or bad trend. What’s clear, though, is that policymakers will have to pay more attention to singles, and not just in an effort to try to get them hitched.
