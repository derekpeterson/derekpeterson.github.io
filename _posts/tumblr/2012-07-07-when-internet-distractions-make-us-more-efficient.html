This strikes me as a similar phenomenon to cities’ ability to catalyze creativity. Being able to process, understand, and set aside the infinite distractions that draw in one’s attention is good, like eating healthy or exercising regularly, but an occasional treat (e.g., mindlessly wandering Twitter and posting to Tumblr, eating dessert, taking a rest day) offers health benefits (mental or physical) and an opportunity to reinforce our positive behavior.
