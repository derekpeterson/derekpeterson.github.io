You never forget
how to be from Michigan when you’re from Michigan.
—

A Primer : The New Yorker

This is an excellent poem about my now-and-forever home, no matter where I go from here. This line in particular rang true for me, a Michigander throughout Europe and the United States and butting up against the Canadian border.
