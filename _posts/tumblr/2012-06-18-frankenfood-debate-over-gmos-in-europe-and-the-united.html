Europe is coming to accept GMO foods as America enters its own period of hysterics over them.

Two notes on the politics:
1.) I understand that GMO sounds creepy, but practically it’s just a more technical and precise way to accomplish the same thing as cross-breeding and hybridization.
2.) The intellectual property apparatus that governs crops is among the worst IP regimes out there, which is saying something.
