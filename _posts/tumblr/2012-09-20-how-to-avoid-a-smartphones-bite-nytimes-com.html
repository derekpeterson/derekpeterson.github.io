Seth Kugel proposes four categories of people, based on their need for being connected to the web: the Blissfully Disconnected, the Semi-Connected, the Moderates, the Power Users, and the Addicts. Personally, I know from experience that I’m a Semi-Connected person. When I’m abroad, I just don’t need to check my email or Facebook constantly, and free WiFi tends to work well enough for me. I do buy a local SIM card for my unlocked phone, but I never seem to actually use it much.
