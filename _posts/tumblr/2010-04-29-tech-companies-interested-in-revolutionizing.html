Tech companies interested in revolutionizing business to make it more about satisfying customers and creating efficiency should get into banking. I can’t think of any other industry in which the entire setup aims to screw the customer in every way possible. To paraphrase John Ensign during the Senate Finance Committee’s recent grilling of Goldman’s executives, at least casinos tell their customers that the house always wins.
