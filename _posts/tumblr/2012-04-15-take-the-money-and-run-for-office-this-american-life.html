This is a particularly excellent episode of “This American Life” on money in politics, from elected officials’ full-time jobs as fundraisers to PACs to campaign finance reform.

In it, Russ Feingold makes an interesting point: with elected officials calling up companies and asking for donations, the current system resembles extortion more than bribery.
